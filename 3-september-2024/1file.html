Title : Falling Tree In Summer

Author : @SarthakAgrawal

3-September-2024

Story :

This is a story of a beautiful jungle and the beautiful tree within it.
People used to come visit that tree to wish for relieving their miseries from their life.
One day a man who is passing by tree said what a beautiful tree and wished i want you to grow with happiness and do not shred your leaves for other's.
Angel heart melted and the beautiful tree transformed in a beautiful lady name amaya , after that amaya decided to go on a journey with this men name sarthak.

Summary :

People do complaints a lot that what actually happining in their lives , and therefore sometimes hurt other people's as well.
Instead of complaining people should fight with their situation and make themselves and other's happy without any greed.
That makes this world beautiful.
Makes the beautiful tree to fall in summer.
